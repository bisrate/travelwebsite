Working for Perfection

Zeleman is an Ethiopian, integrated full service, communications, advertising and production agency. We are the only full service agency with all capabilities built in-house to provide the most efficient and cost effective solutions for our clients. Founded in 2005 by CEO and Chief Creative Director Zelalem Woldemariam, Zeleman currently employs over 120 experienced communications professionals. The agency has long-term relationships with Ethiopia’s biggest brands and international organizations from Diageo, Ethiopian Airlines, Coca-Cola, Tiger Brands, UNICEF, Save the Children and many more. As one of the largest media buyers in Ethiopia Zeleman also has strong relations with all media owners in the country.
